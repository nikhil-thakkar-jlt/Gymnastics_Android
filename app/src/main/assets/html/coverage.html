In general, all activities are covered. This includes matches, training, functions, meetings and the like anywhere in Australia. The Programme provides competitively broad protection across various policies including covereage for 365 days of the year. For further details, please visit our web site.

Personal Injury

Personal Injury cover provides reimbursement of non-Medicare medical costs related to a football injury. Clubs can choose to purchase higher levels of cover (including Loss of Income).

For further details, please visit our web site.

Public Liability

Public Liability covers a range of financial risks such as your club’s legal costs or other costs associated with legal action.

For further details, please visit our web site.

Club Management Liability

Club Management Liability covers a range of financial risks for your club’s directors and officers such as costs associated with legal action if sued for:

  • Negligence, libel, slander, defamation, discrimination, etc.
  • Employment related matters (unfair dismissal)

Club Management Liability can also cover some of the costs associated with an employee stealing funds from the club.

For further details, please visit our web site.

Asset Protect

Asset Protect provides cover for various components of the football club facilities, including damage or theft of contents, stock, equipment, money etc. All clubs (except those in SA and NT) are provided with a maximum of $15,000 Asset Protect cover (limits apply) as part of national programme.

For further details or to purchase higher levels of cover, please visit our web site.

Upgrading Cover

What is it?

The opportunity to purchase additional Personal Injury cover above the standard level.

Who Can Upgrade?

Any Club, League or Association can choose to upgrade one or all components of the Personal Injury policy. Umpiring Associations and Representative Teams can also upgrade their cover.

What Covers Can Be Upgraded?

Non-Medicare Medical: Increase the non-Medicare reimbursements that members receive if injured.

Capital Benefits: Increase the compensation payable for Paraplegia and Quadriplegia events.

Loss of Income Cover: this is not automatically covered in the Base Level but can be purchased by clubs or individuals for additional premium.

For further details including upgrade options and applicable premiums, please visit our web site.

Umpires

All volunteer club umpires are covered under this Programme (to the level of cover taken out by their club).

Umpiring Associations/Leagues who are directly part of a Football League (i.e. umpires are paid by the football league) are provided automatic Personal Injury cover at Bronze Level. These groups can elect to increase coverage to a higher level.

For further details, please visit our web site.
